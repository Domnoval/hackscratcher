Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Scratch Oracle is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • User preferences (budget, risk profile)
  • Win/loss tracking (stored locally on your device)
  • Game recommendations history

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Provide personalized lottery game recommendations
  • Calculate expected value and odds
  • Track your win/loss history (optional)
  • Improve our recommendation algorithms

3. Data Storage

Your personal data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL database) with row-level security policies. Win tracking data can be stored locally on your device if you prefer.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication
  • Minnesota Lottery API: Public game data
  • Florida Lottery API: Public game data
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reporting to improve app stability

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. All lottery game data we display is publicly available information from official state lottery sources.

6. Age Requirement

You must be 18 years or older to use this app. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of win tracking
  • Export your data

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

For privacy concerns or data requests, contact us at:
support@scratchoracle.app
