Delete Your Account

Scratch Oracle

⚠️ WARNING: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone!

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your email address and login credentials
  • Your user profile and preferences
  • Your saved games and recommendations
  • Your win/loss tracking history (if any)
  • All associated account data

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: In-App (Recommended)
  1. Open the Scratch Oracle app
  2. Go to Settings or Profile
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion
  5. Your account will be deleted within 30 days
Option 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app, send an email to:

Email Account Deletion Request

Include in your email:

  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Your account email address
  • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account

Response time: Within 5 business days

Data Retention

  • Personal data: Deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized analytics: May be retained for up to 90 days
  • Legal requirements: Some data may be retained if required by law
Before You Delete:
  • This action is permanent and cannot be undone
  • You will lose access to all your data
  • You can create a new account later if you change your mind
  • Consider exporting your data first (if available)

Questions?

If you have questions about account deletion or data privacy:

  • Email: scratchoracle@gmail.com
  • Privacy Policy: View Privacy Policy
  • Response time: Within 5 business days
